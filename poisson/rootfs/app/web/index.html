Browser Extension Setup

The Poisson browser extension generates noise traffic from your real browser, using your actual cookies and fingerprint. This makes fake traffic indistinguishable from your real browsing.

How it works

The extension opens background tabs to random sites and closes them after a few seconds — generating noise that uses your real cookies and fingerprint, so it's indistinguishable from your actual browsing. It works alongside the add-on, which generates separate noise from your HA server.

It does not read page content, collect passwords, or send cookies anywhere. Auth uses HA's standard OAuth2 — the extension never sees your password.

Installation

  1. Download Extension (.zip)
  2. Extract the zip file to a folder on your computer
  3. Open Chrome and go to chrome://extensions
  4. Enable Developer mode (toggle in top-right corner)
  5. Click Load unpacked and select the extracted folder
  6. Click the Poisson fish icon in your browser toolbar
  7. Paste your Poisson URL and click Sign in to Home Assistant
    --
  8. Log in with your HA credentials — you're done!
